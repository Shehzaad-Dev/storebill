Privacy Policy — StoreBill

Effective date: May 20, 2026

Contact: support@storebill.app

1. Summary

StoreBill is an offline-first invoicing, khata (ledger) and business-card app. Your invoices, receipts and khata data are stored only on your device unless you explicitly export or share them. We do not operate any backend servers for storing your invoices.

2. Data we collect

App data

- Invoices, receipts, and khata ledger entries: stored locally on your device only. Collected: No remote collection.

Third-party SDKs (& what they may collect)

StoreBill does not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs. The app is currently ad-free and does not send your invoice or khata data to advertising networks.

3. Purpose of collection

  • Local storage: all app data is kept on your device unless you choose to export it.

4. Data sharing

We do not share your invoice or khata content with any remote servers. Your data remains on your device unless you export it manually.

5. Your choices

- Export: You can export invoices/ledger entries from Settings → Export Data. Exported files are stored where you choose and may be shared manually by you.

- Delete: You can delete all local data from Settings → Delete All Data. This permanently deletes local invoices and cannot be undone.

6. GDPR / CCPA

If you are in the EU/EEA or California, you have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@storebill.app. We will respond within required statutory timeframes.

7. Security

Invoices and local data are stored in the app's local storage. You are responsible for device-level security (screen lock, backups). If you enable exports, those files are stored on your chosen storage location.

8. Children

This app is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 submitted personal information, contact us to request deletion.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The effective date will be updated. For significant changes we will provide notice in-app or via the app listing.

10. Third-party privacy links

StoreBill does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs at this time. All app data is stored locally unless you choose to export it.

If you have questions, contact: support@storebill.app
